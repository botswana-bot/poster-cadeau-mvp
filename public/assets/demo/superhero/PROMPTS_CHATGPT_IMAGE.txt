PROMPTS — “Superhéros full body (bases pour intégration visage)”

Objectif
- Générer ~10 images de superhéros photoréalistes, FULL BODY, dans des environnements variés.
- Ces images servent de “bases” pour ensuite intégrer / remplacer le visage d’une personne (face swap / inpainting / montage).

Recommandations
- Format: vertical 2:3
- Contraintes clés: head-to-toe visible, mains/pieds visibles, visage dégagé, lumière propre sur le visage, pas d’éléments qui traversent le visage.

----------------------------------------
PROMPT COMMUN (à conserver dans chaque prompt)
----------------------------------------
Photorealistic cinematic still. FULL BODY, head-to-toe in frame, feet visible, hands visible. Subject centered, standing, hero pose. Camera far enough so the entire body fits with margin. 35mm lens look, sharp focus, high detail, natural skin texture. Dramatic but clean lighting. No motion blur. No cropping.
Face clearly visible, unobstructed (no helmet, no mask, no sunglasses, no heavy shadow on the face). Hair not covering the face.
Leave clean space around the head (no sparks/smoke crossing the face). Neutral facial expression, front-facing or slight 3/4. Even lighting on the face.
Original superhero design (not from any existing franchise).
Output: vertical poster, 2:3 aspect ratio.

Optional (if you want extra “face swap friendly”): slightly softer facial detail (not overly stylized), consistent skin tone, no face paint.

----------------------------------------
NEGATIVE PROMPT (si disponible dans ton outil)
----------------------------------------
helmet, mask, sunglasses, face paint, extreme close-up, cropped body, out of frame, blurry, motion blur, lowres, deformed hands, extra fingers, missing fingers, text, watermark, logo
